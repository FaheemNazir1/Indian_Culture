About Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep, a stunning archipelago of 36 islands, is known for its beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and rich marine life. The islands offer a unique blend of traditional culture and natural beauty.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Kavarrati: The capital, famous for its beautiful lagoons and water sports.
  • Agatti Island: Known for its picturesque landscapes and vibrant marine life.
  • Bangaram Island: A serene island ideal for relaxation and adventure.

Cuisines

The cuisine of Lakshadweep is heavily influenced by seafood and coconut. Traditional dishes include Masala Fish Curry, Coconut Rice, and Puttu, showcasing the region's flavors and ingredients.
