Arunachal Pradesh - Cultural Heritage

About Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, known as the "Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains," is famous for its scenic beauty, diverse flora and fauna, and vibrant tribal cultures. It is home to many indigenous tribes, each with their unique traditions and festivals.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Tawang Monastery: One of the largest monasteries in India, significant for its religious and cultural heritage.
  • Itanagar Fort: A historical site reflecting the rich history of the region.
  • Bomdila Monastery: A serene place known for its stunning landscapes and spiritual significance.

Cuisines

The cuisine of Arunachal Pradesh is unique, often featuring bamboo shoots, meat, and regional herbs. Popular dishes include Thukpa, Momos, and Apong (rice beer).

Most Tourist Places

  • Sela Pass
