Assam - Cultural Heritage

About Assam

Assam is famous for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant festivals, tea gardens, and wildlife. The state is home to diverse ethnic communities, and its culture is a blend of different traditions and practices.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Kaziranga National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its population of the Indian one-horned rhinoceros.
  • Majuli Island: The world's largest river island, famous for its Vaishnavite culture and traditional festivals.
  • Ahom Kingdom Ruins: Historical sites that showcase the rich history of the Ahom dynasty.

Cuisines

Assamese cuisine is known for its simplicity and unique flavors. Popular dishes include Khar, Masor Tenga (sour fish curry), and Pitha, a traditional rice cake.

Most Tourist Places
