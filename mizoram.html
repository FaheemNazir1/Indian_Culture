Mizoram - Cultural Heritage

About Mizoram

Mizoram, known for its lush green hills and vibrant culture, is famous for its traditional music, dance, and crafts. The state's rich heritage is reflected in its festivals and the lifestyle of its indigenous tribes.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Aizawl: The capital city known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful landscapes.
  • Hmuifang: A scenic spot known for its natural beauty and cultural significance.
  • Chhimtuipui River: A beautiful river offering scenic views and adventure opportunities.

Cuisines

Mizoram offers a variety of traditional dishes such as Bai, a vegetable dish, and Vawksa Rep, smoked pork, showcasing the state's culinary diversity.
