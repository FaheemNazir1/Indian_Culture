Punjab - Cultural Heritage

About Punjab

Punjab, known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant traditions, is famous for its music, dance, and festivals. The state is home to various religious communities and reflects a blend of cultures.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Golden Temple: A significant spiritual and cultural site, known for its stunning architecture and peaceful ambiance.
  • Jallianwala Bagh: A historical site commemorating the 1919 massacre and a symbol of India's struggle for freedom.
  • Wagah Border: Known for its ceremonial flag-lowering ceremony between India and Pakistan.

Traditional Cuisine

Punjab is famous for its delicious food, including dishes like Butter Chicken, Sarson da Saag, and Makki di Roti, reflecting the agricultural richness of the region.

Arts and Crafts

The state is known for its vibrant folk art, including Phulkari embroidery, traditional pottery, and beautiful woodwork, showcasing the artistic skills of the Punjabi people.
