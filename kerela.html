Kerala - Cultural Heritage

About Kerala

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is famous for its stunning backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. The state is home to various art forms, including Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, and is known for its Ayurvedic practices.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Alleppey Backwaters: Renowned for its serene backwaters and houseboat experiences.
  • Fort Kochi: A historic town reflecting the colonial past and cultural diversity.
  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple: A famous temple known for its architectural brilliance and religious significance.

Cuisines

Kerala is known for its delectable cuisine, including dishes like Dosa, Sambar, and Fish Curry, which highlight the state's culinary traditions.
