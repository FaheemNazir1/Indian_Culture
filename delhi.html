Delhi - Cultural Heritage

About Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and political significance. It reflects a blend of modernity and tradition and is home to several historical monuments and sites.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Red Fort: A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning Mughal architecture.
  • Qutub Minar: The tallest brick minaret in the world, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • India Gate: A war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers.

Cuisines

Delhi's culinary landscape is diverse, offering dishes such as Butter Chicken, Chaat, and Biryani that reflect its rich heritage.
