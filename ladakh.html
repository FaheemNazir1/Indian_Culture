Ladakh - Cultural Heritage

About Ladakh

Ladakh, known for its stunning landscapes and unique Buddhist culture, is famous for its majestic mountains and serene monasteries. It offers a rich tapestry of history and tradition.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Hemis Monastery: The largest and wealthiest monastery in Ladakh.
  • Thiksey Monastery: Known for its impressive architecture and panoramic views.
  • Pangong Lake: A beautiful high-altitude lake with stunning scenery.

Cuisines

Ladakh's cuisine is influenced by Tibetan and Indian flavors, featuring dishes like Momos, Thukpa, and Butter Tea, which reflect the region's culture and lifestyle.
