Dadra and Nagar Haveli - Cultural Heritage

About Dadra and Nagar Haveli

Dadra and Nagar Haveli is known for its lush green forests, tribal culture, and vibrant festivals. This union territory offers a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese influences in its architecture and traditions.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Silvassa Tribal Museum: Showcasing the rich tribal heritage and culture of the region.
  • Vanganga Lake: A picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for relaxation and boating.
  • Dadra Garden: A beautiful garden that provides a serene escape with well-maintained landscapes.

Cuisines

The culinary scene in Dadra and Nagar Haveli features delicious dishes like Prawn Curry, Fish Vindaloo, and Farcha (a spiced fried fish).
