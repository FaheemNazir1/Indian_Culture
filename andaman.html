Andaman and Nicobar Islands

About Andaman and Nicobar Islands

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, known for their stunning beaches and rich biodiversity, are famous for their beautiful coral reefs and vibrant marine life. These islands reflect a unique blend of cultures and are home to indigenous tribes.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Cellular Jail: A historic prison known for its brutal history and beautiful architecture.
  • Havelock Island: Famous for its pristine beaches and lush greenery.
  • Radhanagar Beach: Known for its breathtaking sunset views and tranquil environment.

Local Cuisine

Andaman and Nicobar Islands offer a variety of local cuisines, known for their seafood and tropical flavors.

Most Tourist Places

  • Neil Island
  • Ross Island
  • Long Island

Culture and Language

The culture of these islands is a vibrant mix of various communities, with Hindi, Bengali, and Tamil being the most spoken languages.
