Manipur - Cultural Heritage

About Manipur

Manipur, known for its scenic beauty and rich cultural heritage, is famous for its classical dance forms, traditional crafts, and unique festivals. The state reflects a blend of diverse cultures and traditions.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Loktak Lake: The largest freshwater lake in northeastern India, known for its unique floating phumdis.
  • Khongjom War Memorial: A historical site commemorating the battle against British colonial rule.
  • Shree Govindajee Temple: An important temple reflecting the rich religious heritage of Manipur.
