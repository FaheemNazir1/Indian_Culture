Meghalaya - Cultural Heritage

About Meghalaya

Meghalaya, known for its picturesque landscapes and rich cultural heritage, is famous for its traditional music, dances, and festivals. The state is home to various indigenous tribes, each with unique customs and traditions.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Cherrapunji: Known for its heavy rainfall and stunning natural beauty, including living root bridges.
  • Living Root Bridges: Unique natural structures created by the indigenous Khasi people.
  • Shillong Peak: Offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes and is a significant cultural site.

Cuisines

Meghalaya offers a variety of delicious traditional dishes, such as Jadoh, a rice and meat dish, and Pukhlein, a sweet rice cake, showcasing the state's culinary diversity.
