Jharkhand - Cultural Heritage

About Jharkhand

Jharkhand, known for its rich mineral resources and tribal culture, is a state with diverse traditions, languages, and festivals. The state's heritage is reflected in its folk music, dance, and art.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Netarhat: A hill station known for its stunning sunrise and sunset views.
  • Hundru Falls: A beautiful waterfall offering scenic views and adventure opportunities.
  • Jagannath Temple: A significant temple known for its architectural beauty and cultural significance.

Cuisines

The culinary scene in Jharkhand features traditional dishes like Thekua, Litti Chokha, and Dhuska, showcasing the state's unique flavors.
