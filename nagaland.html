Nagaland - Cultural Heritage

About Nagaland

Nagaland, known for its vibrant culture and colorful festivals, is home to various indigenous tribes. The state is famous for its unique traditions, crafts, and music, reflecting a rich cultural heritage.

Notable Heritage Sites

  • Kohima: The capital city known for its rich history and beautiful landscapes.
  • Khonoma Village: A traditional village known for its rich cultural heritage and eco-tourism.
  • Hornbill Festival: A vibrant festival showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the Naga tribes.

Cuisines

Nagaland boasts a variety of traditional dishes such as smoked pork, bamboo shoot curry, and rice beer, reflecting the state's diverse culinary heritage.
